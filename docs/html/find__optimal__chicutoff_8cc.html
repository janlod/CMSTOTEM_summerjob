Calculate the chi2 cutoff for a desired significance level.

Computes the cutoff on the background histogram (bgHist) such that the probability of observing a background event above this cutoff is less than or equal to significance.

Parameters
bgHistPointer to the background histogram (TH1F*).
sgHistPointer to the signal histogram (TH1F*). Optional here but may be used for reference.
significanceDesired background significance level (alpha).
Returns
The x-value cutoff corresponding to the significance level, or -1 if not found.
